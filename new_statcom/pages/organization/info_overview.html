Services

The mission of the STATCOM Network is to provide pro bono statistical consulting to local nonprofit, government, and community service organizations and to engage students in voluntary, service-oriented statistical consulting and leadership. STATCOM can provide assistance in a wide variety of areas, including but not limited to
  • Study design
  • Data analysis
  • Statistical literature review
  • Simulation Studies
  • Tutorials
STATCOM members generally work in small teams of 3-4 individuals in close collaboration with our clients.

Who We Serve

Please note that we do not provide statistical consulting services to most clients within the University of Washington - such clients are advised to contact the University of Washington Statistical Consulting Service which is designed explicitly to meet the consulting needs of the university community. In addition, we cannot accept clients working for or affiliated with for-profit organizations. Some local companies which offer fee-based statistical consulting services are listed on the Resources page.

Confidentiality

StatCom@UNC maintains a policy of strict confidentiality on all projects. When requested by the client, confidentiality statements will be signed.
